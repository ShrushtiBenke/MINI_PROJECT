About This Project

The Desalination Energy Trade-off Tool is designed to help users understand how different water management strategies can solve water shortages in coastal regions.

Users can explore three major solutions:

• Desalination

• Water Recycling

• Demand Reduction

The tool allows users to create different combinations of these solutions and instantly analyze their:

• Financial Cost

• Energy Consumption

• Carbon Footprint

It also checks whether the selected combination is feasible enough to meet the required water deficit target.

The tool also provides intelligent recommendations based on the user's selected mix of solutions. If the selected combination results in high cost, high energy usage, or large carbon emissions, the system suggests better alternatives for a more balanced and sustainable solution.

• Suggests lower-cost water management strategies

• Recommends reducing high carbon emission options

• Helps users create more energy-efficient combinations

• Warns users if the selected mix cannot meet the water deficit target
