# Desalination Energy Trade-off Tool

## Overview

The Desalination Energy Trade-off Tool is an interactive web-based application designed to help users explore sustainable solutions for water shortages in coastal regions.

The tool allows users to compare and balance three major water management strategies:

- Desalination
- Demand Reduction
- Water Recycling

Users can create different combinations of these solutions and analyze their impact on:

- Energy Consumption
- Carbon Footprint
- Financial Cost
- Feasibility of meeting water deficit targets

---

## Objective

The main objective of this project is to help users understand the environmental and economic trade-offs involved in solving water scarcity problems.

The system supports sustainable decision-making by recommending optimized water management combinations based on cost and carbon efficiency.

- Desalination

3. Install the Live Server extension
4. Right-click `front.html`
5. Click **Open with Live Server**
